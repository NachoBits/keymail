Open Source

KeyMail is completely open source, licensed as ISC. Everyone is free to distribute and modify it as long as you give attribution. Take a look at the code, contributions are encouraged! KeyMail on Github

Secure

KeyMail uses the tried and true GPG standard and all of the cryptography happens on your computer. We never touch your data and we don't want to.

Easy

KeyMail is designed for everyone, you don't even need to change your email address. Don't know anything about encryption? Don't know if an email is safe? You don't have to. KeyMail will let you know.
